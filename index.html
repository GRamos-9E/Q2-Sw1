My Favorite Cartoon/Anime Characters

Picture Brief Description Why I like them
Ice Bear is a character from the show "We Bare Bears". He is one of the 3 bears besides Grizz and Pan-pan. Ice Bear is the silent one among the 3 bears and doesn't talk much. He always starts his sentence saying "ice bear" and only says a few words. He is just like me because I am very white and like to cook.
Peter Griffin is the main protagonist in the adult cartoon "Family Guy". He is the father of his kids Meg, Chris and Stewie and the wife of Lois Griffin. Peter Griffin is very immature as a character, father, husband, and human being with his actions often leading to bad consequences to himself and others. He is mostly known in the series for his wild and often immature antics. There was one episode where he worked for Donald Trump in the White House with the episode ending in an epic 1v1 battle against Trump. He reminds me of myself for having a crazy personality.
Lightning Mcqueen is the main protagonist in the Pixar film "Cars". He was originally an arrogant driver until he got stuck in a town called Radiator Springs and became more humble. Lightning has been one of my favorites and is an important part of my childhood. I remember watching Cars on dvd all the time as a kid. He is who I strive to be, a humble champion.
Otto from the Despicable Me franchise is one of the "Minions". The minions have unknown origins and serve as the henchmen of the main character Gru. Otto reminds me of myself when I was a kid: chubby, clumsy, and being a standout compared to the other kids.
This is Snoopy, a dog from the iconic show "Peanuts". He is friends with the other characters such as Charlie Brown and Lucy. He is usually lazy and mocks Charlie but is a loyal dog to his owner. Most of the people I know love Snoopy and I can see why. He is a cute, little, and adorable dog who will be very loyal to you and be there at your highs and lows. Snoopy is also just soooooooooooooo cute and the man I desire to be: kind, loyal and sweet.
